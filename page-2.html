PROJECTS

project one: interactive audio & visual

A look into the tumultuous relationship between my mother and I.
By following the click-through instructions within the code, users will be able
to hear different songs that I feel best describe our relationship over time.

project two: hypernarrative

This site simulates how I personally experience OCD. Users can click through
the different furniture options within the homepage bedroom and will be
given two options of how to interact. However, no matter what option is chosen,
users are berated and end up in the same place each time: a screen filled with static that
simulates anxiety. This is why I view OCD as a mental illness that gives the illusion of choice.

project three: networked justice

This is an informational site on how one's culture can cause shame,
specifically Chinese culture in my case. Using my own personal experience, I offer
anecdotes that hopefully other POC can relate to, as well as resolutions that
hopefully inspire as well.
